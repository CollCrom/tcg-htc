# How to Play Redline Cindra — Strategy Guide

**Source:** Community article (Calling Memphis 1st place list)
**Author:** Original deckbuilder (Brodie Spurlock piloted to 1st)
**Context:** Redline (all-red) Cindra build, Classic Constructed

---

## Overview

Redline Cindra is one of the most consistent aggro decks in FaB. It plays only red cards and operates entirely pitchless, enabled by Flick Knives + Blood Splattered Vest generating resources on demand. The deck threatens wide combat chains, constant on-hits via Mask of Momentum, and asks difficult blocking decisions every turn.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros
- Highly consistent — no need for red/blue ratio management
- Hands almost always above rate (even pitching red to Kunai is acceptable)
- 1-for-5 and 1-for-7 finishers played for free
- High 3-block count + comfortable defense reactions
- Efficient turns at every hand size
- Multiple on-hit threats = difficult blocking decisions (mistakes are game-losing)
- Excellent Warmonger's Diplomacy user
- Mask of Momentum + Flick Knives is incredibly satisfying

### Cons
- Frostbites (Stalagmite, Blizzard very hard for all-red)
- Poor equipment blocks (equipment is too synergistic to use defensively)
- Linear gameplan (go fast, threaten on-hits)
- Cannot effectively use Arcane Barrier (almost entirely red)

## Equipment Synergy System

The deck revolves around the synergy triangle: **Flick Knives ↔ Mask of Momentum ↔ Blood Splattered Vest**

### Kunai of Retribution
- Primary role: Flick Knives fodder (+1 value per turn)
- Secondary role: unconditional Draconic starter (enables Lava Vein Loyalty etc.)
- Pitching red to Kunai is only -2 value vs playing Head Jab — acceptable
- Ending turn with 1 Kunai: fine. 0 Kunai: dangerous (need starter in arsenal or on top from Rabble)
- One Kunai banished: minimal impact. Both banished: crippling

### Flick Knives
- Flick a dagger as attack reaction → 1 damage "hit"
- **Mask of Momentum synergy**: dagger hit counts as chain-link hitting, preserves hit streak (forces opponent to block again on next link)
- **Blood Splattered Vest synergy**: dagger hit triggers Vest → generates resource → pays for 1-cost finishers without pitching
- Can trigger multiple times per turn if multiple daggers hit
- Avoid blocking with this if possible — increasingly powerful late game
- Always flick unless it would put you to 0 Kunai

### Mask of Momentum
- Draw a card when 3rd+ consecutive chain link hits
- With Flick Knives: dagger hit preserves streak even though it's not an attack action
- **Breakpoint placement**:
  - 1 breakpoint: play 3s until they block, then present the 4
  - 2 breakpoints: chain links 3 and 4 is generally most effective
- Bait 2 Mask blocks → end with Breaking Point / Take the Tempo for additional on-hit
- Ancestral Empowerment blows out opponents who commit resources to block Mask
- Can block with Mask once opponents reach ~6 life (too low for 3 consecutive hits)

### Blood Splattered Vest
- With Flick every turn: essentially an on-demand Tunic counter (3 uses total)
- Enables fully redline deck without pitching
- Don't be afraid to use resources for 1-cost finishers — that's what it's for
- If running low: arsenal cost reducers (Ignite, Art of Dragon: Blood)
- First equipment to block with (after Dragonscaler) once you've gotten 2+ Stain counters

### Dragonscaler Flight Path
- Battleworn 1 = free equipment block without losing anything
- Gives Draconic cards go again (like Snap Dragon Scalers)
- Instant activation: can use AFTER damage dealt and AFTER Mask draw, before go again check (full information)
- Most common use: play two finishers same turn (Lava Burst → Mask draw → Breaking Point)
- Can also give Kunai an extra swing (relevant with Wrath of Retribution for +2 value)

## Core Tenets

### General Gameplay
- Arsenal Draconic starters with natural go again (Brand with Cinderclaw, Ignite are premium)
- 2+ Fealty: Art of Dragon: Blood becomes an excellent "starter"
- 5-card hands help force Mask through → draw card → maintain 5-card hands
- Missing starters: pitch red to Kunai, play rest of hand (most hands still convert efficiently)
- Endgame with Flick Knives is incredibly dangerous at low life totals
- Don't be afraid to block and play small (Ravenous Rabble + Art of Dragon: Blood = 2-card-8)
- Rabble + Flick + Art of Dragon: Fire = 2-card-12

### Blocking Pattern Guide

**Two Chain Link Turns:**
- Never threaten Mask unless Flick + Vest resource → Kunai swing
- 4, 1, 4 can be strong when opponents are out of armor

**Three Chain Link Turns:**
- Opponents block links 1+2, or only link 3
- Ending on a 4 or 5 is most threatening (3-power only demands one card)

**Four Chain Link Turns:**
- If opponent blocks link 1 and you Flick: they only need to block link 2 or 3
- If opponent blocks link 1 and you DON'T Flick: they need link 4 OR both 2+3
- Waiting to Flick is generally better (especially with 4/5 power finisher)

**Five Chain Link Turns:**
- Can use Vest resource to swing Kunai for additional Mask threat
- Opponent blocks link 2 + you Flick: they must also block link 3
- Opponent blocks link 3 + you Flick: they must also block link 4
- Alt: don't Flick, play link 4, Flick + resource, Kunai, play link 6 → Mask again

## Key Card Interactions

### Ignite
- Premium starter: represents 2 chain links (discounts Kunai)
- Cost reduction applies to ALL Draconic activations (Cindra ability, Fealty, Flight Path)
- Fealty sequencing: play Ignite → hold priority → break Fealty → Ignite resolves → next card is Draconic with cost reduction
- Higher priority arsenal as Vest counters are used up

### Fealty & Brand with Cinderclaw
- Cash in for extra value — don't hoard
- Fealty on Art of Dragon: Fire = 2 unblockable damage
- Fealty on Rabble/Fire Tenet = makes it a starter (worth 2-3 value on pitch-to-dagger turns)
- Fealty on Art of Dragon: Blood = starter or extender + cost reduction
- Helps deal with Oath of Loyalty (only Draconic restriction)
- Works through Erase Face (makes cards Draconic regardless)

### Art of Dragon: Blood
- Fine as 0-for-4 chain ender without Fealty
- With Fealty: one of your best starters
- Synergy with Demonstrate Devotion (returns the Fealty used)
- Cost reduction only applies to Draconic cards — can swing daggers between

### Wrath of Retribution
- Best: 3 Draconic links → Wrath → 2 dagger swings → optional 3rd with Flight Path → Flick → Cindra
- Art of Dragon: Blood or Ignite reducing cost twice comes up often
- Blood Runs Deep can give additional swings but usually leaves 0 daggers

### Ancestral Empowerment
- Power card for pushing Mask over blocks
- Best on the chain link where Mask would trigger and opponent blocks exactly
- Only targets Ninja cards (relevant under Erase Face)

### Fire Tenet: Strike First
- Creates breakpoints for Mask (often place on link 2 for link 3 breakpoint)
- Effect applies to next Draconic Attack (can play non-Draconic card between)
- **NOT DRACONIC** (common mistake)

### Take the Tempo & Breaking Point
- Take the Tempo counts HITS not chain links (one attack + Take the Tempo + Flick Knives = 3 hits on 2-card hand)
- Bait Mask blocks early → end with these for additional on-hit
- Lava Burst on 4 → Flick → Flight Path → Breaking Point/Take the Tempo is very powerful

### Yellow Brand
- Pitch to Kunai + use extra resource for 1-cost = 1 less damage but conserves Vest counter

### Loyalty Beyond the Grave
- Card draw decided at start of turn but OPTIONAL — wait for ideal hand
- Excellent for pivot turns to take over tempo
- 6-card hands to push Mask is very powerful
- Enables Lava Burst turns that otherwise wouldn't work
